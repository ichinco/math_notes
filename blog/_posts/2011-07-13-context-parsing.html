--- layout: post title: Context Parsing ---

Parsing loosely refers to an association of l from (Free(L), D) to Tree(Free(L\D)) where for each text M in (Free(L), D), the nodes of l(M) are subwords of M. Here, L is the allowed characters of a language, and D is the subset of L consists of the delimiters.

In language parsing, you also require that a child of a node is a subword of the parent.
